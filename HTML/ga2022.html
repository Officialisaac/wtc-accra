PLUG INTO THE FUTURE

The General Assembly (GA) of the World Trade Center’s Association (WTCA), the most important event on the calendar of the WTCA, will held in Accra, Ghana between the 10-14th April, 2022 The theme for GA 2022 will be “Plug into the future” and will highlight the unique trade and economic opportunities presented by Africa; touted as the world’s next and last economic frontier. This is the first time the general assembly will be held in sub-Saharan Africa. GA 2022 will include conference sessions, field visits, customized B2B and B2G meetings, and networking lunches and cocktails.

Conference Sessions

It is planned that the opening ceremony of GA2022 will be addressed by the President of the Republic of Ghana. The conference session will explore opportunities presented by the Africa Continental free Trade Agreement and other economic movements on the continent. It will also explore opportunities to work with key development partners such as the WB and AFDB

Networking Lunches & Cocktails

Six other African countries will host networking lunches and cocktail where trade and economic opportunities in these countries will be presented. There will also be cocktails and lunches focused on key sectors.

Field & Company Visits

Take a guided tour of West Africa’s biggest industrial zone; visit Ghana’s burgeoning automotive hub and the manufacturing plants to understand what role your business can play in Africa's economic development.

Tours

Enjoy breathtaking touristic trips around the beautiful country of Ghana: beach, wildlife, road trip, cultural artefacts and shopping. There would be touristic visits to other countries from Ghana.

A CONTINENT ON THE MOVE

GA2022 will expose you to the moves and shakers of Africa's economy. Discover investment opportunities across the continent, and acquire valuable market insights from some of Africa’s global business leaders.

Explore opportunities presented by the Africa Continental Free Trade Area (ACFTA) – the largest custom-free trading area in the world with a combined GDP of $3 trillion.

Understand and build beneficial networks in the key economic sectors of Africa.

Explore partnership opportunities with key developmental agencies financing some of the biggest projects across Africa.

Gain insights from Africa's business and thought leaders that are transforming the continents.

Join over 600 participants consisting of potential investors, business leaders, directors and managers of WTCs, company representatives, and accompanying guests from over 90 countries seeking to experience the sights and sounds of Africa.
