INTRODUCTION

WORLD TRADE CENTER ACCRA

Welcome to World Trade Center Accra
WTC Accra currently located on the first floor of the magnificent World Trade Center Building in Accra is a doorway to a world of global trade opportunities for companies in Ghana and the West African sub-region as well as a soft-landing pad for international commercial interests seeking to take advantage of exciting opportunities in Africa. Trade Accelerator. Trendsetter in developing and managing facilities and events. Promoter of innovation and entrepreneurship in Africa

MISSION

To be a doorway to global business opportunities for leading local companies and development agencies leveraging our membership of the WTCA network, trade missions and trade services. To be an effective accelerator of good trade and inclusive economic growth in Ghana and Africa. To become a leading developer and manager of iconic commercial facilities in the sub-region.

VISION

To become a key contributor to prosperity and inclusive economic growth in Africa through the promotion of good trade and business services. To providing business linkages that enhance prosperity for all.


VALUES

At WTC Accra, we have agreed on a shared set of fundamental values that guide us in our day-to-day actions. These values set us apart in excellence from others: Innovation, Integrity, Political non-alignment, Teamwork and collaboration.


LEADERSHIP

RESPONSIBILITY & PROFESSIONALISM

INTERACTION

TEAMWORK SPIRIT AND RELIABILITY

WHY CHOOSE WTC ACCRA?

We are among the leading serviced office providers as well as promoters of international traffic. Not only do we have the capacity to deliver highly tailored services and solutions to suit all tastes and fulfill all needs, but we are also willing to provide unlimited personalized corporate services that cater to your business needs after you have secured the basics. We also ensure value for money with a clear pricing structure by offering world-class, elite and premium services at highly competitive rates.

Honesty and integrity are at the core of everything that we do. We adhere to our ethical standards because we understand the importance of trust in the business world. We also hire the top talented staff and make sure they receive all the necessary training to further develop their skills for the utmost satisfaction of our clients.

WHO DO WE TARGET?

Whether locals, governmental or international business owners, we basically target anyone who is looking to start a business or to expand on an already existing one. Whether you are based in the region or elsewhere, we provide you with a solid operational platform to start conducting business and doing trade missions in Ghana and beyond. You need not to worry about regulations and sponsors. We happily cater to governments, ministries, cultural centers and embassies, investment authorities, and new governmental bodies.

We will be attracting an abundance of investors and business owners who are looking to do business in Ghana. We are prepared to respond and adapt to the increase in demand with the aim to be the facilitator for those people who will be coming in from abroad.
