Welcome to World Trade Center Accra
WTC Accra currently located on the first floor of the magnificent World Trade Center Building in Accra is a doorway to a world of global trade opportunities for companies in Ghana and the West African sub-region as well as a soft-landing pad for international commercial interests seeking to take advantage of exciting opportunities in Africa. Trade Accelerator. Trendsetter in developing and managing facilities and events. Promoter of innovation and entrepreneurship in Africa
